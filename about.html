NATION RURAL LIVELIHOODS MISSION

Arunachal State Rural Livelihoods Mission (ArSRLM) is an autonomous Society registered under the Society Registration Act of 1860 under the aegis of the Government of Arunachal Pradesh. It is the Nodal Agency for implementing the NRLM / Aajeevika schemes in the state. The Mission embodies the principles and vision of NRLM while keeping in mind the unique features of the State. Structurally, ArSRLM comprises of staff at the State, and Block level to cater to the requirements of rural areas across the State.
